add license in your octopress blog

It’s very easy to add license in your octopress blog. just two step:

Step One: Get File

download the following file license to octopress/source/_includes/custom/asides/.

Step Two: Set _config.yml

add custom/asides/license.html in default_asides, as follows:

default_asides: [custom/asides/about.html, custom/asides/weibo.html, asides/recent_posts.html, custom/asides/category_list.html, custom/asides/license.html, ]

Now you have put the license in your blog.
